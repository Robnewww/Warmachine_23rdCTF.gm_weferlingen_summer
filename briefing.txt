<font face="RobotoCondensedBold">GM WAR MACHINE</font><br />
<br />
<font face="RobotoCondensedBold">Objective:</font><br />
Clear all zones from enemy forces present on the map.<br />
<br />
<font face="RobotoCondensedBold">Respawn:</font><br />
- Cleared zones are available as spawn points.<br />
- Deployed Command Vehicles are available as respawn and fast-travel locations.<br />
- Your personal gear is persistent.<br />
- If you die, you respawn with your previous loadout.<br />
- If you reconnect to the session at a later point, you will resume with your previous loadout.<br />
- Flagpoles in friendly zones allow for fast travel.<br />
<br />
<font face="RobotoCondensedBold">Gaining Money:</font><br />
- Capturing zones for the first time awards a big amount of money.<br />
- Destroying enemy aircraft awards a large amount of money.<br />
- Destroying enemy tanks awards a medium amount of money.<br />
- Eliminating enemy forces awards a small amount of money.<br />
- All vehicle crew members are awarded the same amount of money for eliminating enemies. (Kill Assist Reward)<br />
- Your current account balance is shown in the bottom right corner of the screen.<br />
<br />
<font face="RobotoCondensedBold">Spending Money:</font><br />
- Locate the flagpoles on the map. They are indicated by a black triangle.<br />
- If a flag's zone is occupied by friendly forces they can be used to purchase vehicles and equipment, customize appearance and transfer funds to other players.<br />
- You will automatically be moved into any vehicle you purchase.<br />
- Purchased equipment will be delivered to a personal crate that you immediately access.<br />
<br />
<font face="RobotoCondensedBold">Vehicles:</font><br />
- Owned vehicles can be locked and unlocked only by you.<br />
- Command vehicle drivers can deployed the vehicle as mobile spawn point. When deployed, the vehicle cannot be moved.<br />
- Drive any purchased vehicle close to a repair truck or tank to bring up the vehicle customizer.<br />
- Enemy vehicles are locked, you cannot capture them.<br />
- Vehicles can deploy camo nets when stationary. You have to dismount the vehicle to do so.<br />
<br />
<font face="RobotoCondensedBold">Enemies:</font><br />
- Garrison forces occupy enemy zones.<br />
- Smaller patrol forces travel between zones.<br />
- Each zone hosts a reaction force that will reinforce nearby zones under attack.<br />
- The last few remaining units in a city will have one representative highlighted on the map.<br />
<br /><br />
<font face="RobotoCondensedBold">Good luck!</font>
