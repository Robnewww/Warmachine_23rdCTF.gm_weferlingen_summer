The Global Mobilization Warmachine is a persistent Multiplayer game mode.
Your custom loadout will stay with you after you respawn and even after you reconnect.

Reach us on Discord (Click to join directly) if you have issues with the server or other players!

OBJECTIVE


In this game players must capture every location marked on the map.
These locations are towns and bases that are defended by enemy AI.
Conquer these locations to advance the game until all locations are secured.


ASSETS


You will gain cash by eliminating enemies and capturing locations.
At captured locations you will find a flag where you can do the following:

• Equipment Shop - This opens the shop interface where you can purchase vehicles and equipment.
• Transfer Funds - This allows you to send funds to other players.
• Soldier Customizer - This allows you to customize your (camo)face and assign armpatches.
• Squad Management - This lets you form or join a squad.
• Fast Travel - This enables you to telport to already conquered locations.


TIPS


• Kill award money is given to every crew member equally.
• Avoid attacking multiple towns at the same time.
• You can change the visuals of a vehicle in the vicinity of repair trucks!
• You can change the loaded ammo types in the vicinity of ammo trucks!
• Night-time is accelerated time and will last only a short period.


RULES


• No teamkilling!
• No griefing!
• Do not obstruct spawn locations!
• Use the vote kick feature responsibly!
